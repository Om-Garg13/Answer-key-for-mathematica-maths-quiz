🧠 Math Mind Test – Challenge Edition

📘 Answer Key

1️⃣ What is the value of:
3² + (4 × 5) – 6
Answer: 22
3² = 9, 4 × 5 = 20 → 9 + 20 = 29 → 29 – 6 = 22
2️⃣ Find the mistake in this calculation:
(6 + 3) × 2 = 18
Answer: C. It’s actually correct
Parentheses first: (6 + 3) = 9, then 9 × 2 = 18
3️⃣ If a square has a perimeter of 64 cm, what is its area?
Answer: 256 cm²
Side = 64 ÷ 4 = 16 → Area = 16 × 16 = 256
4️⃣ A train travels 120 km in 2.5 hours. What is its average speed?
Answer: 48 km/h
Speed = Distance ÷ Time → 120 ÷ 2.5 = 48 km/h
5️⃣ Logic Puzzle:
“I am a number between 20 and 40. I am divisible by 3, but not by 2.”
Answer: 33
33 ÷ 3 = 11 ✅, not divisible by 2 ❌, range ✅
